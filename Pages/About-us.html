About Us

Welcome to Dr. Klinisch USA, where advanced medical device innovation meets regulatory expertise. As a branch of a globally recognized leader in clinical research, our U.S. operations specialize in integrating comprehensive clinical trial data from our extensive work in India with the stringent requirements of the FDA. Explore our cost-effective and expedited pathways to bring your medical devices to market. We specialise in patient recruitment and retention, resulting in successful clinical studies in shortened timelines.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to streamline the journey of medical devices from concept to market, ensuring safety, efficacy, and compliance with U.S. regulations. Our team of doctors, technical experts, and regulatory professionals is dedicated to providing tailored solutions that address the specific challenges of the U.S. healthcare market.
