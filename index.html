Services we offer

Clinical Evaluation Report (CER)

" Mastering Market Entry - Tailored Reports for FDA Compliance."

Create detailed Clinical Evaluation Reports that align with FDA requirements, ensuring your medical devices are approved swiftly and meet all regulatory standards

Post Market Clinical Follow-up (PMCF)

"Beyond Launch - Ensuring Lifelong Device Integrity."

Conduct thorough Post Market Clinical Follow-ups to monitor and report on your device’s performance in the real world, supporting ongoing compliance and safety.

Summary of Safety and Clinical Performance (SSCP)

"Clarity in Care - Comprehensive Device Insights for Better Health Outcomes."

Provide clear and concise Summaries of Safety and Clinical Performance, offering essential information to healthcare providers and patients alike.

Periodic Safety Update Report (PSUR):

"Continuous Confidence - Keeping Your Product's Promise."

Regularly update Periodic Safety Update Reports to assess and document the long-term safety and effectiveness of your devices.

Clinical Investigation Report (CSR)

"Evidence Excellence - Documenting Success Through Data."

Generate comprehensive Clinical Investigation Reports from U.S. trials, showcasing your product’s efficacy and safety in detailed scientific terms.

Biostatistics and Medical Writing

"Precision in Print - Statistically Sound, Expertly Written."

Offer top-tier Biostatistics and Medical Writing services, ensuring high-quality data analysis and clear, regulatory-compliant documentation.

Design Validation

"Verifying Vision - Ensuring Your Design Meets Demand."
